Aphtus Productions LLC Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information inside our games and applications.

Data Collection and Use

Aphtus Productions LLC only collects data about users for the purposes of third party advertisements. No other data is collected, stored, or used in any way apart from as described in the section labelled "Third Party Data" below.

Third Party Data

Some of Aphtus Productions LLC's free apps use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data about you to customize advertising based on your interests. This data may include your device's advertising identifier, IP address, and device information. We do not control or influence the data collection and use practices of AdMob. To opt out of personalized google ads, or for more information, please visit the following links: Google Ad Center Google Ads Policies For more information about Google related policies and privacy practices, please visit these links: Google Privacy Policies Google Partner Sites Policies

Child Related Data

Any free apps targeted at children will serve only child-friendly ads from admob. Otherwise, we in no way knowingly collect, store, use, or transfer data from children of any age.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. All links will be updated and the modification date at the bottom of the page will reflect the date of the most recent change.
